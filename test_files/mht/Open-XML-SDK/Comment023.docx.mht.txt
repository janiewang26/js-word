On the Insert tab, the galleries include items that are designed to coordinate with the overall look of your document. You can use these galleries[AA1][AA2][AA3] to insert tables, headers, footers, lists, cover[AA4] pages, and other document building blocks. When you create pictures, charts, or diagrams, they also coordinate with your current document look.You can easily change the formatting of selected text in the document text by choosing a look for the selected text from the Styles gallery on the Home tab. You can also format text directly[AA5] by using the other controls on the Home tab. Most controls offer a choice of using the look from the current theme or using a format that you specify directly.To change the overall look of your document, choose new Theme elements on the Page Layout tab. To change the looks available in the Style gallery, use the Change Current Style Set command. Both[AA6][AA7][AA8] the Themes gallery and the Styles gallery provide reset commands so that you can always[AA9] restore the look[AA10][AA11][AA12] of your document to the original contained in your current template.[AA1]Testing CommentsParent Comment 1[AA2R1]Child Comment 1[AA3R1]Reply 2 to Comment 1Parent [AA4]Comment 2Parent [AA5]Comment 3[AA6]Parent Comment 4[AA7R6]Reply 1[AA8R6]Reply 2[AA9]Parent Comment 5[AA10]Parent Comment 6[AA11R10]R1[AA12R10]R2
